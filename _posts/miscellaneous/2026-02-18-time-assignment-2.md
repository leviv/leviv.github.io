The only non-computer clock I have access to is a microwave. Not a ton to say about it. To set the time

1. Press dedicated 'time' button. Numbers flash
2. Enter the time via the number pad (12 hour time)
3. Press confirm/start

I usually set it 1-2 minutes late so I trick myself to being on-time to things.
